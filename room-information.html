Parameter Ideal Range Description
Temperature 20°C - 25°C The temperature should be within this range to ensure comfort and prevent overheating or chilling.
Humidity 30% - 50% Maintaining proper humidity levels helps prevent the growth of mold, bacteria, and viruses. It also promotes respiratory health and prevents dryness or excess moisture in the air.
Air Quality 0 - 50 AQI (Air Quality Index) The Air Quality Index measures the level of pollutants in the air. A value within this range indicates good air quality with minimal pollutants, ensuring a healthy environment.
